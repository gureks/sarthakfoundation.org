Stories of Sponsored kids!

Ritika. from XYZ Center

Hi!! My name is Ritika and I'm 6. I'm the only kid of my parents. I live with my grandma. I wonder why papa always fought with mummy but now they don't live together anymore. Both grandma and mummy love me more than anyone else but they were not able to continue my schooling. So I had to drop it. But now I've started it again. I'm going to school again with my friends and this school is even better than the last one. None of the ma'ams ever punishes any kid. My ma'am says that I have the best handwriting in the class. So I just love it here. Now I can see my dream of becoming a teacher turning into reality. Touchwood!!
