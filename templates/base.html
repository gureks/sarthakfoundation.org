SARTHI

Sarthi is a Hindi word for Charioteer. A Sarthi drives you, guides you and hods your hand when you need a direction in your life. By becoming a Sarthi, you will give the kids the hope to realize their dreams that they have just started seeing for themselves. Become their hope - become their Sarthi - Help them study.
